Thanks for your input @chadmira ! We have been taking the cumulative hospitalization numbers from the Arkansas DOH page https://www.healthy.arkansas.gov/programs-services/topics/novel-coronavirus and the number for Total Tested for COVID-19 from the state dashboard https://adem.maps.arcgis.com/apps/opsdashboard/index.html#/f533ac8a8b6040e5896b05b47b17a647 Arkansas updated its testing numbers after our publish time cutoff. If a state updates testing numbers after that time, we catch that update on the following day. So far, the historicals we keep seem to agree with this pattern. We’re working internally to best represent this data as well as shifting our time to catch states that update only slightly after our publish time.
