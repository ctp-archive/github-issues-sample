Hi,

I am looking at the database for the number of cases in the US and either there are some internal inconsistencies or I do not understand what ‘total’ means.

My understanding was that total was positive+negative+pending. This is true for all dates but the following ones: 10,11,12 and 17 of March. Importing the dataset into a pandas dataframe called covid19_usa_daily and running

covid19_usa_daily['total']==(covid19_usa_daily['positive']+covid19_usa_daily['negative']+covid19_usa_daily['pending'])

you see that this is not always true. Also, if you compute the difference between left-hand and right-hand sides you see that the left one is always smaller, which should not be correct.
