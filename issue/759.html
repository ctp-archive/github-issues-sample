Issue: Upon further outreach and review of the document linked in sources, we have updated our understanding of how NC reports total test results. NCâ€™s total tests include results from all tests administered for one person and have reported that the unit for total tests is specimens. Therefore, we should copy over all values from Total PCR Tests (People) to Total Tests (PCR) and report all values for total tests in Total Tests (PCR) from here on out.

Sources: https://www.ncdhhs.gov/news/press-releases/ncdhhs-corrects-covid-19-testing-totals-after-identifying-labcorp-reporting
