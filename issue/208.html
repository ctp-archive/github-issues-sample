I live in Washington State where the website of my county health department states: “The number of individuals tested is provided by the Washington State Department of Health and is an under-count of the number of people tested in Clark County. The state data does not include people receiving rapid-result testing or who are being tested at the point of medical care, which are becoming more common methods of testing in Clark County.”

I know that you are aware of this issue, but I ask that you please say more on the website about the extent to which you are finding ways to collect data on point-of-care / rapid testing. As you are aware, over the next month such testing is likely to account for an increasing percentage of tests.
