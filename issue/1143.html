Issue number 1143

Open in Github

State: NH

Dates affected: See attached file.

Describe the issue: New Hampshire sometimes updated after the time of our daily update. In order to ensure our timeseries is as complete as possible, we patch these late updates in our historical data. Additionally, on March 14, 2020 through August 8, 2020, we often caught their update on the day after it was published, resulting in gaps in our data, and their data not all being on the same timeseries. This will be corrected in this patch as well.
