Thank you writing, @kbalzli

Different states update the public presentation of their data at different times throughout the day. Some are very regular in when they make updates, some update only when there is specific news, some have a daily report. Additionally, the reported numbers are often compiled from testing done the previous day. The schedule for our screen captures is standardized for all of the pages, and while not instantaneous, we will capture the cumulative updates over the course of a day.

The screenshots we take are so that we can go back and double check historical data. The reported values which we include in the available data are collected three times a day, because of the varied reporting schedule from states, and are based on the state sites at the time, not the screen captures.
