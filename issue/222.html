Why do the number of U.S. deaths on your Covid19 Tracking site differ so much from the Johns Hopkins Coronavirus site? For today (4-18-20) your site reports 34,139 U.S. deaths, while the Johns Hopkins site reports 38,910 U.S. deaths. I’ve been watching them both and they seem to have matched fairly well until about 2 days ago. Hi Brian,

The reason for the discrepancy is that NYC on 4/14 added more than 3700 presumed deaths retroactively to its deaths count. https://www.reuters.com/article/us-health-coronavirus-usa-idUSKCN21W20G

We have not yet added these presumed historical deaths into our reports.
