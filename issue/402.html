Hi @lkhemani thanks so much for your comments. We collect data manually from the states’ data dashboards or department of health websites. We take automated screenshots of those websites several times a day. The screenshot times are listed and linked here: https://covidtracking.com/data/state/georgia#historical. For many states, the screenshot timing does not occur at the same time as our manual data entry. It seems that the discrepancy that you are seeing on April 27/28 is due to this. This might be the same explanation for your question about the number of cases reported from the 12th to 13th. Can you let us know which month this was in?
