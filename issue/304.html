How is Washington State grade a B?. Hospitalization data incomplete - all hospitals do not report every day, # is meaningless. Hospitalization data does not include % capacity. Hospitalization # didn’t even begin to reported until April 9 (backdated to April 6) - as a result of proclamation by DOH Sec. Weisman on April 2 (17 days after initial lock down). Presentation of data is incoherent for average person. Takes a 25 min youtube video to interpret data on BI platform and website. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jdZwOU1qdWQ. Data reporting has been inconsistent - days without any information. Some counties are reporting probables as confirmed and state is counting them as confirmed (Yakima - these are not confirmed by testing, wording that cases include probables was removed on April 19th, but data still includes probables).
