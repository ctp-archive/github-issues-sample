Issue: On September 18, 2020 Arkansas stopped reporting Total/Positive/Negative Antigen tests (specimens) and began reporting Total/Positive/Negative Antigen tests(people). We will be keeping the time series as provided by the state, but removing the carried over values from September 18, 2020, forward.
