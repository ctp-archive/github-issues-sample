CA recent numbers are strange

Issue number 141

Jmuccigr opened this issue on April 4, 2020 at 3:31 pm

Open in Github

Something seems odd with the recent CA numbers:

  1. They got 47,000 pending tests back today and they were effectively all negative. In fact their positive increase was lower than yesterday.
  2. Even ignoring the returned pending cases, they added 30k new test results when the most they’ve been able to do before is 7k (once, over a month ago).
  3. For the third straight day, their totalTestResults were an even multiple of 100.
  4. For the past two days, their totalTestResultsIncrease was likewise a multiple of 100.
  5. Their pending cases have also been -00, but I had figured that was just routine rounding.

I feel a little conspiracy-addled even writing this, but #1 seems so incredible to me that the others seem worth mentioning as well. This isn’t a direct issue for the project, but perhaps someone has a way to ask them about this.
