Issue number 99

Open in Github

On the Hawaii Department of Health website sourced for covidtracking data, the category “Pending” does not refer to people in Hawaii who have been swabbed and are still awaiting test results. It means people who have tested positive but whose county of residence has not yet been confirmed.

Just FYI.
