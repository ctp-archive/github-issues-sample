[AR] Antigen totals fix

Issue number 792

karaschechtman opened this issue on August 26, 2020 at 1:47 pm

Labels Data quality

Open in Github

State or US: AR

Describe the problem AR shared a value for its total number of antigens verbally in a presser on 8/20 (10,358: see 2:33 in https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_cqwYiMx7EY) significantly higher than what we found on the slides they post on their website a few days later on 8/25 (3,610 tests in https://www.healthy.arkansas.gov/images/uploads/pdf/briefing_slides_8_25.pdf). We have conducted outreach to ask why this discrepancy exists and for a full time series of the metric. In the mean time we are carrying over the old value (1836) from 8/20-8/24 and using 3,610 from 8/25 onward.

Link to data source https://www.healthy.arkansas.gov/images/uploads/pdf/briefing_slides_8_25.pdf
