Show daily, rather than cumulative, figures on history pages

Issue number 51

breyed opened this issue on March 25, 2020 at 7:14 am

Open in Github

How many cases we have now matters much more for decision-making than how many we’ve ever had.

For example, an increase in daily positive results relative to daily total results is generally indicative of the disease spreading, and likewise a decrease indicative of receding. With all-time cumulative numbers, that kind of analysis doesn’t hold.

The cumulative is useful for a retrospective. At some point, that’s what we’ll want to do. But first we’ve got to make smart trade-offs to curb the disease. Let’s focus the data there.
