I understand. Working with spotty data. Still, I see you have added columns for current in addition to cumulative. Kudos on a spectacular effort to gather information and present it to the public. The CDC should be requiring states to report data for current and cumulative for: tests, ill, hospitalized, recovered, ICU, ventilators. If states don’t have the data, they are woefully unprepared to predict equipment and personnel needs.
