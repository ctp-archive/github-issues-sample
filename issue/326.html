Suprised this sight doesn’t track these data points… considering there are sites out there that report these numbers daily.

Navajo Nation should certainly be tracked: https://www.ndoh.navajo-nsn.gov/COVID-19

US Military, the DoD provides daily updates: https://www.airforcemag.com/snapshot-dod-and-covid-19/

Also, Veterans Affairs also provides updates: https://www.accesstocare.va.gov/Healthcare/COVID19NationalSummary

These totals are not included in state totals… and account for 15,898 cases to date and 547 deaths.

If you wanted, you could also include Federal Prisons. https://www.bop.gov/coronavirus/

I know this is a state by state tracker but these people are Americans and they should be included in your numbers. At least the Navajo Nation should - as these numbers were removed from New Mexico’s totals early on and given their own category.
