The discrepancy began with the 4/23/2020 death total, which from that day on, appears to have been calculated as the sum of both the Maryland ‘confirmed’ and ‘probable’ death counts. Prior to 4/23/2020, the death numbers reported on this site were consistent with the MD ‘confirmed’ death count.

Hi @metaconcept

Thanks for reporting this issue!

The CDC issued a guidance on 4/14 that case counts and death counts include confirmed and probable cases There is more information about the definitions of confirmed and suspected here. We have standardized this guideline across all the states that report both numbers. We have also updated Maryland’s death counts to include ‘probable’ deaths beginning on 4/15 when the state began reporting this number. We’re in the process of making ‘probable’ and ‘confirmed’ deaths more transparent.
